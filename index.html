Instructions
  • Avoid the mob of dancers by moving your mouse to a safe location.
  • Dancers that catch you slows you down, so be careful!
  • Collect D-O-N-N-A letters to shove these dancers away!
About
Celebrating the Golden Era of Donna Cruz's career, Donnafied: Kapag Tumibok ang Puso is "flash-mob simulator" where you have to dodge hordes of wannabe dancers as they dance to the beat. Game design is based on the song's lyrics.
